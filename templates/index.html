About

About The Application

Online marketplaces such as Amazon allow people to share their experiences about purchased products using textual comments known as product reviews. Online reviews from previous consumers are key information sources for consumers and marketers. Fake product reviews on e-commerce websites mislead consumers and undermine trust in online marketplaces. Detecting and eliminating fake reviews is essential to ensure genuine feedback and maintain the platform's integrity.

How To

Check Out These Steps

1. Our Home Page welcomes you with an easy-to-use interface.

2. Copy the URL of the product page from Amazon (or other supported sites) that you wish to analyze. Paste it into the provided input box on our website.

3. Hit the "Analyze- button to start the review detection process. Our advanced AI algorithms will begin scanning and evaluating the reviews for authenticity.

4. Within a few seconds, see the results! We'll display a detailed breakdown of our analysis, including the percentage of fake and genuine reviews, and provide an overall grade (A-F) for the products review quality

5. Use our insights to decide whether to proceed with your purchase. Feel confident knowing you are making a smarter choice!
